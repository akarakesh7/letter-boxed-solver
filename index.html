Frequently Asked Questions

What is Letter Boxed? +

Letter Boxed is a popular word puzzle from The New York Times. Players must form words using letters arranged around a square, with consecutive letters coming from different sides. The goal is to use all letters in as few words as possible.

How does this solver work? +

Our solver uses advanced algorithms and a comprehensive English dictionary database to find valid word combinations that connect all letters while following the game's rules. It prioritizes solutions with the fewest words.

Is this solver free to use? +

Yes, our Letter Boxed solver is completely free to use with no registration required. You can solve unlimited puzzles without any cost.

Does this work for the daily NYT puzzle? +

Absolutely! Our solver works with any Letter Boxed puzzle, including the daily challenges from The New York Times. Just input the letters from today's puzzle.

Can I use this on my phone? +

Yes, our solver is fully responsive and works perfectly on all devices including smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.
